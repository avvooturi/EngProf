Project 1 - AI-Assured IC Design

Posted on: November 5, 2024

Category: Tech | Year: 2024

Problem Statement

    Integrated Circuit (IC) Design, and Implementation is time consuming

    - Most of the steps require heavy optimization involving np-hard problems

    CHIPS and Science Act reshores IC design and manufacturing to US

    - > 50,000 semiconductor engineers will be required within next 5 years

    Chip placement impacts the subsequent steps/quality of the chip

    Takes weeks/months for skilled engineers  DARPA IDEA program

Solution

    PlacePro, No-human-in-the-loop IC design by reinforcement learning (RL)

    Cuts down placement time from weeks/months -> hours/days

    Utilizes human-inspired topological and testability features during placement
